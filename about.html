About Us

High quality with tradition from the north of Germany: For 140 years, the name Jahncke has stood for innovation in the fruit processing industry.

Paradise Fruits Freeze Dried (Paradiesfrucht GmbH) is a young and dynamic company that has developed into a leading global specialist in the field of freeze-drying. We offer an extensive portfolio of high-quality freeze-dried products as well as flexible solutions for industrial applications.Carefully selected, freshly harvested and frozen raw materials are gently dehydrated using sophisticated technology to remove the water bound as ice, while the cell structure of the original product is retained. Although freeze-dried fruits are very small and light, studies show that the antioxidant secondary plant compounds are retained in almost the same level as fresh fruits.

Further services include contract drying options for our customers. With state-of-the-art technology and expanding capabilities, we ensure consistent high quality and continuous supply. Our freeze-dried products contain no additives – just 100% exquisite fruit flavour.
